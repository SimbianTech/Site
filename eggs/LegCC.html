The ChampionCraft Legacy
ChampionCraft was a popular SMP realm in its early days turned nations/factions realm in its later days.

The realm was full of a bustling community with tons of life which always had something going on such as wars, controversies, religions or unfortunately, hackers.

The realm had many enemies and many friends, over 500 in-game members, and reach into YouTube, Reddit, and Facebook.

The realm lasted approximately 7 months and went through 3 separate seasons. The realm was owned by Chaamp10n for the majority of its run but was also looked after by SimplyGam3r as well.

Although ChampionCraft is over, its legacy continues with its community moving to a new realm known as “The Metroplex,” run by @Deleted User
