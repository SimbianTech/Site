ChampionCraft Season 1

Season 1 also known as ChampionCraft 1 was the first realm in the ChampionCraft series and the third realm in the timeline. This season began as a sucessor to Whaly Whale's Free Survival Realm and started on December 30th 2019. Season ended mostly due to lack of intrest from players and was succeeded by Championcraft 2.

Spawn

Town Hall from the inside

Spawn of this season was the center of almost all action as the concept of this season was based around towns similar to it's predecessor thus everything was here, untill at a later date Town 2 and Town 3 were introduced.

Town Hall

Town Hall from the outside

Town hall was built verry early into the season, a hundred or so blocks from spawn and it also allowed players to exchange items using the chests near its fountain and request help from others. it also featured a few public furnaces and crafting tables. It had an Announcements board where important changes were announced incase players didnt have Discord.

Comunity Gardens

Comunity gardens were built right next to spawn go give new players an easy access to food.

Town 2

Town 2

Town 2, later renamed to Town 3 was built on January 18th 2020 and it expanded living area and introduced the ChampionCraft Mall where players could buy plot and offer various items using whatever items the shop owners decided to use as currency though the standart currency was gold nuggets. Outside the mall there was another shop named "KMart" but it was never finnished.

The ChampionCraft Mall

Inside the ChampionCraft Mall's first floor

The mall was opened on February 16th 2020 had 3 main floors, the first floor conatined Sadwichking9's and Mega gamer6576's Bar, Mega gamer's Construction facility, Pope's Restaurant, GamesLines's hardware store, hecturrrr's pawn shop and CokoTokos Eggcelent Eggs as well and an escalator to go to the second floor. The second floor contained CocoToco's Flower Store as well as an unfinnised Pet Shop and a few more open plots, it also had a jukebox in between the escalator with some music discs to play. The third floor was unfinnshed and it only contained a few open claims purchasable for 3-6 stacks of gold nuggets and a level 4 beacon built by Gam3r proving players with regenaration and resistance. The mall also featured a glass pane door for an enterence and escalators which would slowly take you between floors, both powered by command blocks. Shops in the mall also had to pay taxes which were 20% of the profits that the shop has earned within an unspecified duration of time.

Town 3

Town 3

Town 3 was created near the end of the season to attempt to bring back some activity to the realm, Town 3 was also named Town 2 and as a side effect Town 2 was renamed to Town 3, but I will refer to it as Town 3, Town 3 didnt have any actual builds untill the 2024 rerelease and the builder of it is unknow but most likeley its the realms owner Chaamp10n which follows the towns rustic theme. It also has a few info boards near its nether portal. Rules of this town were the same as the other towns howerver there additional rules like the towns buildings have to be bellow Y 110 and there has to be no modern looking builds. there is also 9 diamond block wall with a sign on it that says "the next person that sees this tell me... -Phugnugs".

The Nether

Nether hub

The nether had a hub where all the Ice highways branched out to all the players bases and the 2 additional towns. Main use of the nether hub was using the 5 nether highways to travel between players bases and Town 2 and 3, player that had their bases set up up to 2000 blocks away from town could also get their highways constructed for free. Players could build in the hub and customise their highway branches but not the main highway.

Nether Highways

There were 5 highways it total, 2 in the east, 2 in the noth and 1 in the west, south only had the exit to the nether. The first eastern highway had exits to YkVae's, Gam3r Legend's and creepo123's bases as well as Town 2 and 3 and a base for sale. The second eastern highway had exits to Sandwichking's, GamesLines's, Simbian Gaming's, Galaxitalix's and Chaamp10n'sand Drilxn's bases. The first nothern highway only lead to OG Phantom's base, while the second lead only to N3WK13TheBomb's base. The eastern highway only lead to Enderboyz's base.
